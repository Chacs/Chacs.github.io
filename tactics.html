Playing Tactics

We are a very strong defensive team, due to our athleticism and communication. We play with a lot of aggression and generally tend to play “under” downfield cutters, trying to mess up offensive flow and forcing them to attempt hi-risk deep looks. We put a lot of emphasis on handler defense where we play hard “no-around” on force-sideline sets OR playing behind the dump handler on force-middle sets. We also like to throw a lot of defensive looks at teams with two different zone styles and a lot of force-middle on man points.
